Our Products & Services

BIOMEDICAL SUPPLIES, CONSUMABLES & EQUIPMENT

We intend to grow the value of our business by developing safe and effective products to meet our client needs. Hidalgo procures, stores and distributes drugs, medical supplies and consumable to be used in research facilities, public health centers, and institutions.
Our objective is to ensure availability of medical supplies, equipment and consumables in the right quantity, with the acceptable quality, to the right place and customers, at the right time and with optimum cost to our esteemed clients. We are certified by the National Drugs Authority and registered with the PPDA.

Setting up and stocking school laboratories

Hidalgo Investments has vast expertise setting up laboratories of learning institutions/schools with the latest safety and quality standards.
We supply and install lab equipment for chemistry, biological and physical science laboratories.

Installations of firefighting systems

Our fire alarm system consists of smoke detectors, heat detectors, glass break switches, and blazers which are connected to control panels.

In case of a fire outbreak, the siren/bell sounds to alerts the user. We supply smoke detectors, fire extinguishers and alarms.

We offer servicing of all fire equipment, refilling of powder, foam, carbon dioxide and water fire extinguishers.

Service, Repair and Maintenance of Lab Equipment

Hidalgo Investments Limited has a team of skilled bio-tech engineers with vast experience installing, repair and maintenance of a wide range of lab equipment. We also do calibration of all lab equipment.

Among other things, we install, repair and calibrate; centrifuges (Refrigerated and Non Refrigerated), Freezers, Liquid Nitrogen tanks, Fridges, Biosafety cabinets, Liquid Nitrogen Production Plants, Water bathes, Water distillers, Safety showers, Air conditioners, Vortex Mixers etc
